What's A Hardiness Zone? Hardiness Zone Map

Find the Best Plants Based on Hardiness Zones

The USDA Plant Hardiness Zone Map is the standard by which gardeners and growers can determine which perennial plants are most likely to thrive at a location. The map is based on the average annual extreme minimum winter temperature.

To get a closer look at the map, check out the Zone Map linked in the navigation bar above.

To find a list of plants that will do best in the Plant Hardiness Zone of your location, enter your zip code in the box on the main page, and a list of plants best suited to your zone will appear. Happy Gardening!
